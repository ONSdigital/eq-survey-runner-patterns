eQ Colours

Contrast between foreground and background colours are tested to ensure WCAG AA compliance. Where colour is used to draw attention to an item (eg panels) and insufficient contrast exists between a background colour and element colour, additional techniques may be required to provide support for users unable to perceive the colour.
