<h2 class="neptune">Headings</h2>
<p>By default, headings have all their font-sizes reset to `1rem`, with the typographic hierarchy enforced by the 'type scale', eg:</p>
<h1 class="jupiter">Heading 1 (Jupiter)</h1>
<h2 class="saturn">Heading 2 (Saturn)</h2>
<h3 class="neptune">Heading 3 (Neptune)</h3>
<h4 class="venus">Heading 4 (Venus)</h4>
<h5 class="mars">Heading 5 (Mars)</h5>
